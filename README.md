# insurance-policy-claims-analysis

# Project Overview
Portfolio of business intelligence project using Power BI to analyze insurance policies and claims data,
helping stakeholders track performance, identify risks, and optimize decisions.

# KPI Requirements:
- **Total Policies** - Active customer base size.
- **Total Claim Amount** - Overall financial impact of claims.  
- **Claim Frequency** - How often claims are filed. 
- **Average Claim Amount** - Typical claim size to assess risk exposure.
- **Gender-wise Total Policies** - Customer distribution by gender. 

# Visualization Highlights  
- **By Car Use** – Personal vs. commercial policies and claims.  
- **By Car Make** – High-risk brands with more claims.  
- **By Coverage Zone** – Regional analysis of policies and claims.  
- **By Age Group** – Claim frequency by customer age brackets.  
- **By Car Year** – Effect of vehicle age on policies and claims.  
- **By Kids Driving** – Household risks with young drivers.  
- **By Education & Marital Status** – Customer profiling with heatmaps. 

# Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** - (Data modeling, DAX, dashboards)  
- **MySQL** - (Data extraction & queries)
